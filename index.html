On the day

Here's what you can expect to happen on the day:

  • location_on You'll arrive at UserLab and let reception know you're here to take part in a focus group with WhatUsersDo.
  • mode_edit One of our research team will come down and meet you, pay you £70 in cash, and take you through to the session.
  • people During the session, you'll be sat round with 7 other people, and two of our lovely researchers.
  • laptop_mac The researchers will be asking your opinions on some things relating to football, and will ask you to carry out a couple of tasks.
  • doneThat's it! Once your session is over, you'll be given some information to take away.

If you can't make it

Plans change, and unexpected things come up, so you might need to let us know you can't make it.

It's not a problem if this happens, but please let us know as soon as possible so that we can find someone to come in instead.

If you know more than 24 hours beforehand, then please email users@whatusersdo.com.

If something happens on the day, then please call Alex or Hazel on 07549 224230.

Getting in touch
